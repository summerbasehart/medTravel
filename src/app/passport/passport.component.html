First Time Applicants

If you have never applied for, been denied, or held a US passport, you will need to apply, either by mail or in person.

Step One: Application

To begin a passport application, please use the US State Department form filler, located here

Alternately, you may begin your application at your local post office, and schedule an appointment here

Step Two: Documents

To apply for a new passport you will need the following documents:

  • Application For A U.S. Passport (DS-11)
  • Passport Photos
  • Citizenship Evidence
  • Photo Identification
